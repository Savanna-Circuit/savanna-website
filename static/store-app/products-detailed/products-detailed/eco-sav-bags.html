Frequently Asked Questions

Are these bags food-safe?

Yes, they're certified food-safe and made from recycled HDPE food-grade plastic. Suitable for grains, beans, dried products, and other agricultural storage.

How long do they really last?

With proper care, 200-300 uses (3-5 years typical). That's equivalent to 200-300 single-use bags, making them economically superior.

Can I stack them full?

Yes, each bag holds up to 100kg. When properly stacked (aligned vertically), they support multiple bags above them safely.

What about pests?

The sealed design protects contents from insects and rodents. For maximum protection, store in a cool, dry location off the ground.

Can I get different sizes?

Yes, sizes range from 5L to 50L. Most farms buy mixed kits (combination of sizes) optimized for their storage needs.

What if a bag gets damaged?

Small punctures can be patched with waterproof tape. For major damage, we offer replacement bags. The 200+ use lifespan accounts for normal wear.
