Frequently Asked Questions

Is pasteurized milk safe for consumption?

Yes, absolutely. Pasteurization is the gold standard for milk safety worldwide. It eliminates harmful bacteria while maintaining nutritional value. All major milk supplies are pasteurized.

How long does pasteurized milk last?

Pasteurized milk lasts 7-14 days refrigerated, 3-4x longer than raw milk. This dramatically reduces spoilage losses and enables access to distant markets.

Can I produce cheese from pasteurized milk?

Yes, cheese production actually benefits from pasteurized milk (more consistent results). The system includes heating options optimized for cheese making.

What certifications do I need?

The Eco-Sav is HACCP and ISO 22000 certified. Most buyers require these certifications, and our system documentation helps you achieve dairy board compliance.

How often does it need maintenance?

Regular cleaning (daily), minor maintenance quarterly, professional service annually. The automated clean-in-place system makes maintenance straightforward.

What's the processing capacity?

The system processes 50-500L/hour depending on model. The 500L/hour version handles large-scale commercial production, while smaller capacities suit medium farms.
