MaziwaPlus Prechillers 300-1000L

Hybrid solar-powered milk chiller

Entry-level hybrid milk chiller designed for small to medium-sized dairy operations. The MaziwaPlus Prechillers efficiently cool and store milk while significantly reducing operational costs through solar-assisted cooling technology.

  • Capacity: 300 - 1000 Liters (Customizable)
  • Power System: Hybrid (Solar + Grid/Backup)
  • Cooling Technology: Advanced refrigeration with solar pre-cooling
  • Build Quality: Stainless steel exterior, insulated interior
  • Best For: Individual farmers, small collection centers

Cost Savings
