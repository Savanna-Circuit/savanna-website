System Overview

MaziwaPlus DMS is a cloud-based platform that brings modern data management to dairy operations. Track every aspect of your business from milk production to profitability, with real-time insights accessible via web dashboard or mobile app from anywhere.

Core Features

  • Production Tracking: Daily milk yield, collection, quality metrics
  • Animal Records: Breed, health, vaccination, breeding history
  • Feed Management: Inventory, costs, consumption tracking
  • Financial Reports: Revenue, expenses, profit margins, ROI
  • Quality Assurance: Temperature, testing, certification compliance
  • Staff Management: Worker assignments, tasks, performance
  • Market Integration: Price tracking, sales orders, buyer management

Cooperative Features

  • Member Tracking: Individual farm records & production
  • Aggregation Reports: Group-level analytics
  • Payment Distribution: Auto-calculate payments per member
  • Quality Control: Enforce cooperative standards
  • Collective Reporting: Buyer/government compliance
  • Communication Tools: Alert/notification system
  • Training Library: Knowledge base for members

Analytics & Insights

  • Production Trends: Monthly/seasonal analytics
  • Cost Analysis: Per-liter production cost
  • Profitability: Revenue vs. expenses comparison
  • Benchmarking: Compare against industry standards
  • Forecasting: Predict future production/profits
  • Custom Reports: Export data for banks/investors

Mobile App Capabilities

  • Field Entry: Record data without internet (sync later)
  • Real-time Updates: View latest data on smartphone
  • Quick Actions: Record collection, quality issues, health alerts
  • Photo Capture: Document conditions and issues
  • Notifications: Alerts for important events
  • Offline Mode: Works without internet connection
  • iOS & Android: Both platforms supported

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need internet to use the app?

The mobile app works offline - you record data and it syncs when you reconnect. Perfect for farms in areas with intermittent internet. The web dashboard requires internet access.

Is my data secure in the cloud?

Yes, enterprise-grade encryption protects all data. Automatic daily backups prevent data loss. We comply with international data protection standards.

Can I export my data?

Absolutely - export reports as PDF or Excel anytime. You own your data and can request full dataset export whenever needed.

How many farmers can use one account?

Unlimited users on cooperative plans. Assign roles (owner, worker, manager) based on permissions needed. Each user gets own login.

Can it connect to my cooling system?

Yes, MaziwaPlus Pro coolers have built-in IoT. Other cooling systems can be integrated via API. Contact us for specific compatibility.

What's the training process?

Free onboarding includes live training, video tutorials, and documentation. Dedicated support during first month. Additional training available for cooperatives.
