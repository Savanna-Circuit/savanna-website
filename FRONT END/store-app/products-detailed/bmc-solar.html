Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work on cloudy days?

The integrated battery stores excess energy during sunny days. On cloudy periods, the system draws from battery reserves, providing 24-48 hours of operation depending on cloud cover. Rare extended cloudy periods can be managed with compact gas backup (optional).

What is the payback period?

Most farms achieve full ROI in 4-6 years. After year 6, you enjoy essentially free cooling for 20+ more years. Total 25-year savings reach KSH 2-3 million compared to grid electricity.

Can I expand capacity later?

Yes, the modular design allows for panel and battery expansion as your farm grows. Contact us for upgrade assessments and pricing.

Is maintenance required?

Minimal maintenance: quarterly panel cleaning and annual professional inspection. Battery systems are sealed and require no servicing under normal conditions.

What happens if batteries fail?

LiFePO4 batteries are extremely durable with 20+ year lifespan. Even at 25 years, they retain 80% capacity. If replacement needed, it's covered under extended warranty plans.

Does weather affect operation?

The 48-hour battery autonomy handles most weather variations. Extreme extended cloud cover (rare in Kenya) would require brief grid connection or gas backup activation.
