Frequently Asked Questions

Can a single person operate a 50L can?

When full (55kg), a 50L can requires two people to carry or should be loaded via truck. The industrial handles support two-person carrying, but most operations use forklifts or truck loading.

How many fit on a standard truck?

Typically 6-8 units fit per truck when stacked properly (2 layers of 3-4 units). This delivers 300-400L per truck load, making transport highly efficient.

Is this worth it for smaller operations?

For operations moving less than 50L regularly, consider the 25L. The 50L is ideal for cooperatives with 30+ farmers or commercial operations. Compare per-unit cost vs. usage volume.

What's the maximum distance it can travel?

With pre-chilled milk and good insulation, reliable cooling is maintained for 200+ km transport. On extended routes, plan mid-journey stops to re-chill if needed.

Do you offer fleet maintenance packages?

Yes, we offer fleet care programs including annual inspection, replacement parts, and bulk wash service. Contact our sales team for fleet operation packages.

What wholesale pricing is available?

Fleet orders (5+ units) receive significant discounts. Contact us with your projected volume for custom fleet pricing and terms.
