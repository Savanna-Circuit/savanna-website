Overview

Since our inception, Savanna Circuit has been dedicated to improving the livelihoods of smallholder farmers across Kenya. Our innovative solar-powered cooling solutions have enabled farmers to reduce post-harvest losses, increase their income, and access new markets. Through partnerships with local organizations and governments, we have expanded our reach to over 50,000 farmers in 10 counties in Kenya.
