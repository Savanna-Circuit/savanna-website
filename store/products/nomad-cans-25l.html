Frequently Asked Questions

Is this better than two 10L cans?

Yes - one 25L can is more cost-effective than two 10L cans (lower total price, better cooling efficiency per unit, easier to manage). It's ideal if you have regular need for this volume.

Can a single person carry it when full?

Full (28kg) requires two people or placing on a cart. The ergonomic handles support two-person carrying. This design encourages cooperative spirit.

How much longer does it stay cool than the 10L?

The 25L maintains cool temperature 2-4 hours longer due to thicker insulation and thermal mass. This makes it ideal for longer transport distances.

Can multiple cans be stacked on a truck?

Yes, when full and properly secured. Don't stack empty cans as the lids may dent. Stacking capability makes truck transport very efficient.

What's the cooperative bulk pricing?

Larger orders (10+ units) receive special cooperative rates. Contact our sales team for exact pricing based on your group size and commitment.

How do we decide between 25L and 50L?

Choose 25L for medium cooperatives (5-10 farms) or 50L for larger groups (15+ farms). Many groups use a combination of both sizes for flexibility.
