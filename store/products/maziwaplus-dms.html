MaziwaPlus DMS

MaziwaPlus DMS is a cloud-based, mobile-integrated platform for real-time monitoring, route management, and digital records across the dairy supply chain. It connects milk transporters, processors, quality officers, and administrators into a single, traceable ecosystem.

Quick Specs

  • Platform: Cloud + Android mobile
  • Offline: Works offline with auto-sync
  • Modules: Registry, Logs, Payments, Analytics
  • Users: Cooperatives, processors, agents
  • Integration: IWD, Nomad, BMC data compatibility

Data Capture

  • Volume and temperature readings at point of collection
  • Source attribution and time-stamped entries
  • Can traceability and operator IDs
  • Route performance metrics and spoilage events

Mobile Application

  • Android app for field users, designed for offline-first operation
  • Automatic synchronization when connectivity is available
  • Role-based access for agents, clerks, and supervisors
  • Prompted workflows to reduce data entry errors

Benefits

  • Traceable records enabling transparency and accountability
  • Operational insights via dashboards and analytics
  • Seamless integration with cooling equipment for data logging
  • Digital payments and receipts for faster payouts

Integration

  • Compatible with IWD for dispensing logs
  • Interfaces with Nomad Cans workflows and mobile units
  • Logging for Bulk Milk Chillers (BMCs) volumes and temperatures
  • Export support for cooperative and processor reporting

Deployment & Support

  • Training for field and admin users
  • Configuration of routes, users, and collection points
  • Ongoing maintenance and feature updates

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the mobile app work offline?

Yes. Field users can capture records offline and the app auto-syncs when connectivity is restored.

Which modules are included?

Farmer & Route Registry, Milk Collection Logs, Payments & Records, Analytics Dashboard, and Mobile Access.

Who uses the system?

Cooperatives, processors, agents, quality officers, and administrators across the dairy value chain.
