Nomad Can 10L

Insulated, food-grade milk can with integrated thermocore cooling

Nomad Can 10L

Nomad Cans are patented, insulated food-grade containers designed for the safe transport of milk. Built for modular use with Ice Water Dispensers (IWD) or mobile trikes, these cans maintain optimal temperature using a chilled glycol thermocore insert.

Quick Specs

  • Size: 10 Liters
  • Weight: 3.6 kg (with Thermocore)
  • Cooling Method: External contact with chilled glycol water inside thermocore
  • Material: Food-grade Aluminium, HDPE cladding, double-insulated body
  • Lid: Leak-proof, wide-mouth opening
  • Use Cases: On-farm cooling, decentralized collection, pre-cooled storage

Superior Benefits

  • Functional: Anti-spill, leak-proof, lockable design
  • Integrated Cooling: Solar-powered ecosystem; no external refrigeration needed
  • Sustainability: Carbon-neutral cooling vs. diesel refrigeration
  • Operational: Reduced spoilage; improved reliability over long distances

Weight Comparison

  • Aluminium (w/lid): 2.4 kg (10L)
  • stainless steel (no lid): 3.0 kg (10L)
  • Nomad Can (with Thermocore): 3.6 kg (10L)

Compatibility

  • Ice Water Dispensers (IWD) for refilling Thermocore
  • Nomad EV/D mobile cooling and dispensing
  • MaziwaPlus DMS for collection logs and traceability

Handling & Care

  • Rinse and sanitize after use; allow to dry fully
  • Inspect lid seals periodically
  • Follow loading guidelines for optimal cooling
