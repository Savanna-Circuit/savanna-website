Our Story

Research (2017 - 2019)

Initial research and development focused on understanding the challenges faced by small-holder farmers in AFrica and exploring innovative solutions for dairy, Fish and solar cooling.

First Model Deployment & Distribution (2020 - 2022)

Launched the first hybrid milk chiller model, began distribution to farmers, and established key partnerships for scaling impact across regions.

Continued Partnerships & Improvements (2023 - To Date)

Expanding Cold Chain product offerings such as SOLAR-THRIVE, deepening partnerships with processors, cooperatives and micro enterprises, and continuously improving technology and service delivery to maximize farmer impact.
