EcoSav Bags

EcoSav Bags are durable, insulated carry packs designed for transporting fish and produce in cooled conditions. Used together with ladder packs filled with chilled glycol water from Ice Water Dispensers or mobile trikes, they preserve freshness, reduce spoilage, and deliver high-quality products to market.

Key Features

  • Thermal Insulation: Multi-layer walls maintain cold for 8–12 hours
  • Flexible Carry Options: Shoulder straps, side handles, or top-load design
  • Waterproof, Food-Safe Inner Lining: Hygienic and easy to clean
  • Rugged Outer Fabric: Dust- and weather-resistant for rural/market conditions
  • Custom Sizing: Configurable capacities starting from ~60kg

Product Overview

Whether strapped on a motorbike (boda), placed on a boat, or carried by hand, EcoSav Bags enable reliable cold transport for fisheries and produce. They integrate seamlessly into Solar Thrive cooling ecosystems using Ice Water Dispensers and mobile trikes.

Technical Specifications

  • Cooling Type: Passive cold retention using glycol ladder packs

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do the bags keep contents cold?

With properly loaded glycol ladder packs, EcoSav Bags maintain cold conditions for 8–12 hours depending on ambient temperatures and handling.

What sizes are available?

Capacities are customizable. Typical configurations start from ~60kg, with options tailored to boats, motorcycles, or hand-carry needs.

Are the bags food-safe?

Yes. The inner lining is food-safe and waterproof, and designed for easy cleaning to maintain hygiene.

What do I need to operate them?

You’ll need chilled glycol water to charge ladder packs—available from Ice Water Dispensers (IWD) or mobile trikes in the Solar Thrive ecosystem.
