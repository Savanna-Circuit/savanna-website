Frequently Asked Questions

What can I dry besides grains?

Almost anything - fruits, vegetables, herbs, fish, leather, coffee, spices. Temperature-adjustable design accommodates different products' requirements.

How much can I earn from dried products?

Typically 200-400% markup over fresh price. Example: 100kg fresh tomatoes → 15kg dried worth 3-4x the fresh value. Early adopters in regions are earning significant premiums.

How long does drying take?

1-3 days typically depending on product thickness and moisture content. Herbs dry fastest (1 day), vegetables/fruits 2 days, grains 1-2 days at optimal temperature.

Can I operate during cloudy periods?

Limited capacity but possible with some battery backup. Most farmers plan batches during sunny seasons or use reduced capacity mode on cloudy days.

Do I need certifications for export?

Yes, but we provide guidance on HACCP compliance and quality standards. The drying process itself is certified; you need product certification from relevant authorities.

What's the market demand?

High demand for certified dried products in export markets, hotels, and specialty food channels. We help connect farmers with buyers through our network.
