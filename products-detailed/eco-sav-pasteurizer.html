EcoSav Pasteurisers

EcoSav Pasteurisers enable safe, efficient milk processing, extending shelf life and meeting food safety standards. Built for small-scale and cooperative-level dairy production, they ensure high-quality pasteurised milk and dairy products ready for market.

Key Features

  • Electric or hybrid heating (grid + generator)
  • 100–500L batch capacities
  • Food-grade stainless steel, easy-clean design
  • Digital thermostat with pasteurization cycle automation
  • Seamless integration with Bulk Milk Chillers and MaziwaPlus DMS

Frequently Asked Questions

What power options are available?

Units are available in electric or hybrid (grid + generator) configurations for flexibility in grid-limited contexts.

How is temperature controlled?

A digital thermostat manages pasteurization cycles automatically, ensuring consistency and safety.

Can batches be tracked?

Yes, EcoSav Pasteurisers integrate with MaziwaPlus DMS to track batches and maintain digital records.
