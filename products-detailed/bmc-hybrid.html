Bulk Milk Chiller (Hybrid)

Designed for cooperative hubs and milk collection centers, Bulk Milk Chillers rapidly chill and store large volumes of milk, safeguarding quality from farm to processor. Powered by solar with hybrid backup options, they provide dependable, hygienic bulk milk handling in off-grid or grid-limited areas.

Key Features

  • Glycol-based active chilling with rapid pull-down
  • Hybrid power: solar-first with grid/generator backup
  • Food-grade stainless steel with multi-layer insulation
  • Digital temperature control and monitoring
  • Integrated with MaziwaPlus DMS for volume and temperature logging
  • Customizable capacities from 200L to 2000L
