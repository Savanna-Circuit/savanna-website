Frequently Asked Questions

Does it require water input connection?

Yes, the IWD connects to an existing water source (well, tap, tank). Water passes through a filter then cooling system before dispensing. No electricity required beyond solar panels.

Can it operate during cloudy weather?

Yes, battery backup provides 24-36 hours of operation. On extended cloudy periods, water remains cool from the passive insulation system.

How many people can it serve daily?

A 200L system serves 50+ people daily (4 cups each) or 100+ people at smaller portions. Capacity scales with your usage needs.

Is the hot water function necessary?

Not essential but useful for coffee/tea facilities. Many farms use cold-only mode to save costs. Upgrade anytime later.

What about maintenance?

Filter replacement every 6 months and annual professional maintenance. The system is designed for minimal upkeep.

Can schools get special pricing?

Yes, we offer educational institution discounts and can help with funding applications. Contact our community programs team.
