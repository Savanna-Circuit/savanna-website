Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Pro and Standard models?

The Pro model features IoT smart monitoring, higher capacity (200-2000L vs 300-1000L), superior temperature precision (¬±0.5¬∞C vs ¬±1¬∞C), extended warranty (3 years vs 2 years), and automated analytics for data-driven farm management.

How does the mobile app work?

The MaziwaPlus Pro app provides real-time monitoring via 4G/WiFi connection. View current temperature, receive alerts, access historical data, monitor energy consumption, and manage settings from any smartphone. Works on iOS and Android.

What happens if internet is unavailable?

The system functions normally without internet. Data is stored locally and syncs when connectivity is restored. Critical alerts (like temperature drops) are sent via SMS for reliability.

Can I upgrade from Standard to Pro?

Yes, we offer trade-in programs and upgrade packages. Contact our sales team to discuss options for migrating your existing system or upgrading capacity while maximizing your investment.

What is the expected ROI?

Most Pro installations achieve ROI within 18-24 months through energy savings, improved milk quality, reduced spoilage, and optimized operations. Exact timeline depends on your farm size and current operating costs.

Is training included?

Yes, comprehensive training covers system operation, app usage, maintenance procedures, and troubleshooting. We provide on-site training and ongoing video tutorials for your team.
