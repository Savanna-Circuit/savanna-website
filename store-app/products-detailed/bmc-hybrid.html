Frequently Asked Questions

Why choose BMC Hybrid over other options?

The BMC Hybrid prioritizes proven reliability and durability in harsh African climates. It uses industrial-grade components that minimize maintenance and work reliably even with power fluctuations, making it ideal for remote farming areas.

How often does it need maintenance?

Maintenance is minimal - monthly visual inspection and annual professional servicing. The simplified design means fewer failure points compared to systems with advanced electronics.

Can it handle power outages?

Yes, the hybrid system seamlessly switches between solar and grid power. During extended outages, the battery backup maintains cooling for several hours depending on system size.

What is the typical lifespan?

With proper maintenance, expect 12-18 years of reliable operation. The industrial-grade construction ensures longevity in demanding conditions.

Is there service support in remote areas?

Yes, our regional service centers and trained technicians provide support across Kenya and East Africa. Response times average 24-48 hours even in remote locations.

What about spare parts availability?

Standard compressor and electrical components are locally available in most regions. We maintain distributed spare parts inventory for faster service and reduced downtime.
