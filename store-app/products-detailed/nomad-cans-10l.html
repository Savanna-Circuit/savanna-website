Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it keep milk cool?

8-12 hours typically. Pre-chilled milk at 4¬∞C will remain at safe temperature for this duration. Using ice packs can extend cooling time by 2-4 hours.

Can I use it in very hot weather?

Yes, but cooling time reduces. Start in early morning when temperatures are cooler, and consider using ice packs for longer transport times in extreme heat.

How do I maintain it?

Simply wash with clean water after each use and let dry. The passive design has no moving parts to maintain. Store in a cool location when not in use.

Is it food-safe for direct milk contact?

Yes, the interior uses food-grade polyurethane materials that meet dairy industry standards. Always wash before use and keep clean.

What's the typical lifespan?

With proper care, 5-8 years is typical. The durable construction handles frequent use in farm environments.

Do I need ice packs?

Not required, but highly recommended for longer transports or hot weather. They cost little and significantly extend cooling time.
