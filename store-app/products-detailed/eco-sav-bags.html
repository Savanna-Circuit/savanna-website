EcoSav Bags

EcoSav Bags are durable, insulated carry packs designed for transporting fish and produce in cooled conditions. Used together with ladder packs filled with chilled glycol water from Ice Water Dispensers, they preserve freshness, reduce spoilage, and deliver high-quality products to market.

Quick Specs

  • Insulation: Multi-layer; 8–12 hours cold retention
  • Cooling Medium: Glycol ice ladder packs
  • Lining: Food-safe, waterproof inner lining
  • Exterior: Rugged, dust- and weather-resistant fabric
  • Carry: Shoulder straps, side handles, top load
  • Sizes: Custom; starting from ~60kg capacity

Benefits

  • Reduced spoilage and waste
  • Improved product quality and shelf life
  • Portable and versatile for different transport modes
  • Low operating cost when paired with IWD hubs

Compatibility

  • Ice Water Dispensers (IWD) for chilled glycol water
  • Nomad EV/D mobile distribution
  • EcoSav ladder packs (glycol ice packs)

Support

  • Guidance on pack loading and route planning
  • Cleaning and maintenance best practices
  • Replacement liners and ladder packs available

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do the bags keep contents cold?

With properly loaded glycol ladder packs, EcoSav Bags maintain cold conditions for 8–12 hours depending on ambient temperatures and handling.

What sizes are available?

Capacities are customizable. Typical configurations start from ~60kg, with options tailored to boats, motorcycles, or hand-carry needs.

Are the bags food-safe?

Yes. The inner lining is food-safe and waterproof, and designed for easy cleaning to maintain hygiene.

What do I need to operate them?

You’ll need chilled glycol water to charge ladder packs—available from Ice Water Dispensers (IWD) or mobile trikes in the Solar Thrive ecosystem.
