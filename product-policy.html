Product Policy

Last updated: January 2025

At Savanna Circuit Technologies, we are committed to delivering high-quality solar cooling and agricultural processing solutions. This policy outlines the warranty, return, and support guidelines for our products to ensure transparency and customer satisfaction.

1. Warranty Information

We stand behind the quality of our manufacturing. Our warranty covers defects in material and workmanship under normal use conditions, provided that installation and maintenance are carried out by authorized Savanna Circuit technicians.

Warranty Periods

  • Structural Stainless-Steel Fabrication: 2 Years
  • Rational Machines: 2 Years (if installed by Savanna Circuit)
  • Electrical Fittings: Not covered under standard warranty due to external power quality factors. We highly recommend installing voltage guards.

Warranty Conditions

  • Warranty is valid from the date of delivery.
  • Warranty is applicable only if the full cost of the equipment has been paid.
  • Warranty is void if installation, service, or repair is performed by unauthorized personnel.
  • Warranty covers the replacement of spare parts but excludes transport, food, and accommodation costs for technicians.
  • Damages caused by negligent use, wear and tear, or faulty supply of utilities (gas, water, power) are not covered.

2. Returns and Refunds

If you need to return a product, please adhere to the following guidelines to facilitate a smooth process:

Return Window

  • Notification: You must notify us of any intent to return an item within 5 days of delivery.
  • Return Period: Items must be physically returned to our premises within 15 days from the date of delivery.

Return Process

  1. Contact us to obtain a Return Goods Authorization (RGA) number. Returns without an RGA form will not be accepted.
  2. Ensure the item is in good working condition. We may take up to 3 business days to inspect returned items.
  3. Present the original receipt and invoice.

Refund Terms

  • Refunds: A 5% deduction applies to the total price to cover administrative costs.
  • Credit Notes: A 3% deduction applies if the item's seal is broken. Further depreciation charges may apply based on usage.
  • Refunds are processed within 30 days of receiving the returned item.
  • Transport costs for returns are borne by the client.

3. Installation & Site Readiness

To ensure optimal performance of your equipment, proper installation is critical:

  • Pre-installation: Our engineers will conduct a site inspection. Installation begins only when the site is confirmed ready.
  • Client Responsibilities: The client is responsible for providing necessary utilities (stabilized power, soft water, drainage, gas lines) within 1.0M of the equipment location.
  • Completion: Installation is deemed complete once the equipment is commissioned and put into use.

4. Maintenance and After-Sales Support

We are dedicated to the longevity of your equipment:

  • Preventive Maintenance: We provide 2 free preventive maintenance services within the first two quarters after installation on turnkey projects.
  • Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC): After the free service period, we strongly recommend signing an AMC to ensure continuous operation and revenue generation.
  • Spare Parts: We maintain a stock of essential spare parts. We suggest clients invest 5% of the contract value in spare parts inventory for immediate availability.
  • Training: We offer training for your technicians at our Nairobi workshop to handle minor repairs and maintenance.
