How IWD Powers the Ecosystem

The Ice Water Dispenser (IWD) is the heart of our last-mile cooling solution. It generates and stores ice, providing a ready source of cooling for Nomad Cans and Eco-Sav Bags, ensuring produce and dairy remain fresh during transport. This decentralized cooling hub, powered by solar energy, also supports the charging of devices for MaziwaPlus DMS data entry and powers our Electric Mobility solutions, creating a fully integrated, sustainable ecosystem for farmers.
