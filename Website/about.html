Our Story

Research (2017 - 2019)

Initial research and development focused on understanding the challenges faced by small-holder farmers and exploring innovative solutions for dairy management and solar cooling.

First Model Deployment & Distribution (2020 - 2022)

Launched the first hybrid milk chiller model, began distribution to farmers, and established key partnerships for scaling impact across regions.

Continued Partnerships & Improvements (2023 - To Date)

Expanded product offerings, deepened partnerships, and continuously improved technology and service delivery to maximize farmer impact.

Mission

To empower youth and smallholder farmers with innovative, sustainable agricultural solutions that increase productivity, improve livelihoods, and create economic opportunities.

Vision

A thriving agricultural ecosystem where farmers have access to cutting-edge technology, quality products, and expert support to build sustainable, profitable farms.

Our Values

  • Innovation: Continuous improvement in product and service delivery
  • Sustainability: Environmental and economic responsibility
  • Farmer-Centric: Solutions designed with farmer needs in mind
  • Impact: Measurable improvement in farmer livelihoods
  • Integrity: Transparent and ethical business practices

Our Approach

Technology

Cutting-edge solar, IoT, and cloud-based solutions designed for the African context and smallholder farmer needs.

Service

Expert field support, training, and customer service that ensures farmers can maximize the value of our products.

Data

Data-driven insights that help farmers make better decisions and optimize their operations for maximum impact.

We work closely with farmers and partners to understand their challenges, co-develop solutions, and provide comprehensive support throughout their journey. Our integrated approach ensures that technology, service, and data work together to deliver measurable results.
