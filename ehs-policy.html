INTRODUCTION

Since 2017, Savanna Circuit has remained steadfast in its commitment to sustainability and ethical practices. This is in line with our purpose to transform the lives of the farmers we serve through reduction of losses, maintaining the quality of their produce and providing innovative solutions that incorporate IoT. Savanna Circuit Technologies (SCT) is closely intertwined with the solar powered industry in Kenya specifically it’s utilization in the dairy industry through the manufacture of a wide range of solar powered dairy equipment. We have based our excellence on meeting the best practices in the market in which we operate.

Our strategic focus on these areas has continued to position our business in an upward trajectory for resilience and growth and long-term value creation.

IMPACT STORY

Post harvest loss is one of the greatest challenges our agricultural sector faces accounting for 20%-30% losses in harvested produce. This is brought about by various factors such as poor storage facilities, inadequate handling techniques, lack of proper transportation among others. SCT noticed the market gap and stepped in with our innovative solutions that has brought positive impact to the farmers in terms of income and reduction in post-harvest losses.

Our long-term plan has been to have all levels of farmers have access to our products by making it affordable across all levels of farmers, from the small scale to the large-scale farmers. In relation to this, we introduced the Nomad cans that are affordable and cuts across all levels of farmers.

ENVIRONMENT

The health of our environment directly impacts everyone and individual and collective steps need to be taken in order to reduce our environmental footprint. Savanna Circuit’s does provide the guideline for our environmental priorities, with a focus on conservation of resources and waste minimization from our operations.

We recognize the importance of climate change as a defining issue which requires our collective action to mitigate the risks it poses. Our aim is to proactively reduce our impact on the environment, champion a proactive stance in protecting our planet & shape a sustainable future.

Carbon emissions are a major contributor to climate change and as SCT we are actively implementing strategies to embark on with the aim of reducing our carbon footprint. These efforts include:

  • Reduction of business travels where necessary by prioritizing virtual over physical engagements leading to a reduction in our CO2 emissions associated with business travel.
  • Installation of LED lighting at our offices and also sourcing part of our production inputs locally.
  • Installation of solar equipment to our clients that require them. Our Solar Thrive project is 100% solar powered for its operations.
  • Prioritizing suppliers that have put in efforts to reduce their carbon emissions.

Waste Management and recycling

We have been working on adopting a circular economy approach whereby we identify and reduce our sources of waste, recycling and reusing more and purchasing products that are sustainable where possible.

We have been finding use for our waste stainless steel sheets which are crucial in our production process. We have been able to reuse these sheets in part of our manufacturing process for the Nomad Cans while maintaining the quality of our final product. The waste HDPE used on Nomad cans are collected and recycled at local facilities that deal in plastic recycling.

Governance Structure

At Savanna Circuit believe that successful implementation of the policies and frameworks are as a result of our governance structure which not only reinforces our commitment to transparency and accountability but also oversee our strategy come to life for efficient operations.

Our aim is to ensure transparency and accountability in regards to our pillars of governance. Various reporting frameworks will be required to ensure comprehensive reporting that aligns with the expectations of different stakeholders.

The findings of our ESG report will guide our long-term sustainability goals which will be integrated in our strategies and business objectives. The board, which comprises of the management, will approve the policies and programs that support the ESG and climate change matters and the committee will be responsible for overseeing its implementation ensuring we stay abreast of material risk exposures including those that may arise due to climate change.

SCT management is essential in implementing the ESG and climate change strategy. The Board of Management approves the strategy and commitments and oversees the implementation. Dedicated ESG champions will be selected from our various departments to ensure that we are able to identify and mitigate the ESG related risks and help formulate strategies to tackle them. There will be frequent meetings to track the progress on ongoing steps taken.

Environmental and Social management systems

Our Environmental and social due diligence tool will be formulated and incorporated to identify, address and mitigate potential risks effectively to show our commitment and proactive approach to integrating comprehensive risk management practices into its operational framework.

A strong emphasis on capacity will be made to ensure the data collected in the process is core in our operations. This will show our promise of transparency and continuous improvement in our Environmental, Social and Governance initiatives. This will not only show our commitment to align with local and global standards but also reaffirm our role in fostering a sustainable future. Part of our plan is to ensure that we utilize solar energy in our operation.

Climate risk management

SCT acknowledges the significant risk posed by climate change which include the recurrent droughts and floods which impacts the different sectors of our economy. We will work on showing our commitment by providing regular updates on our plans to mitigate this with our operations.

We are keen to tackle climate related risks and opportunities by ensuring that we have solar powered equipment to reduce our emissions. We ensure this by having conducting assessments to our clients to ensure that we are able to provide the best services to them at a considerable cost. We will continue to seek collaborations with various stakeholders including customers, investors, stakeholders, employees and the broader society to support this and move towards a low carbon future.

Stakeholder Engagement

We prioritize stakeholder engagement by adopting an all-inclusive approach by valuing their feedback. Regular reviews will be conducted to meet the diverse interests of our stakeholders.

Material Topics

We prioritize both financial performance and integration of ESG principles in our daily operations. Our materiality assessment process allows us to identify and prioritize the sustainability topics that we identify to play a role in our long-term success. The process will be kicked off by collecting insights to pinpoint key areas where we have the greatest potential to impact both society and our business.
