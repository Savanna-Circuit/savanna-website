SOLAR THRIVE COLLECTION

Nomad Cans

Patented, insulated food-grade containers designed for the safe transport of milk, maintaining optimal temperature using a chilled thermocore insert.

Eco-Sav Bag

Durable, insulated carry packs designed for transporting fish and produce in cooled conditions, preserving freshness from farm to market.

Ice Water Dispenser (IWD)

The heart of our ecosystem. It generates and stores glycol-cooled water at -30Â°C, powering Nomad Cans and Eco-Sav Bags. This decentralized cooling hub supports MaziwaPlus DMS integration, creating a fully integrated, sustainable ecosystem for farmers.

MaziwaPlus DMS

A cloud-based, mobile-integrated platform for real-time monitoring, route management, and digital records across the dairy supply chain.

Electric Mobility

Sustainable, low-cost transport for last-mile delivery of agricultural products, fully integrated with our solar ecosystem.
