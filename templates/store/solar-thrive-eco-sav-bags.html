Eco-Sav Bags

Portable thermal energy storage solution

Innovative phase-change thermal storage bags for portable cooling and heating. Perfect for transporting temperature-sensitive products like milk, vaccines, medicines, and food. Cost-effective, reusable, and environmentally friendly alternative to traditional ice packs and coolers.
