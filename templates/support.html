Troubleshooting Common Issues

My cooler is not cooling properly. What should I do?

Check that solar panels are clean and receiving sunlight. Ensure battery connections are secure. Verify ambient temperature isn't extreme. Contact support if issues persist.

How do I update my MaziwaPlus DMS?

Updates are automatic for cloud-based subscriptions. Check the Settings menu for the latest version. For mobile apps, update from your device's app store.

What's the expected lifespan of my equipment?

Solar cooling units typically last 10+ years with proper maintenance. Dryers last 8-12 years. Software subscriptions continue as long as you maintain your subscription.

How often should I service my equipment?

Annual professional maintenance is recommended. Regular cleaning and basic inspections should be done quarterly. We offer maintenance packages at affordable rates.
