Welcome to Savanna Circuit Technologies

We are a youth-led manufacturing and last-mile distribution company providing hardware and software solutions for use in post-production. Our intervention targets the areas out of the reach of post-harvest programs and solutions.

We build solar cooling milk chillers, solar dryers, and dairy management solutions for post production across the supply chain for aggregation, chilling on transit and performance management.
